Discover the numerous different lawns and their
unique attributes throughout Plants vs. Zombies.


Front Lawn Day

A Day based lawn with five lanes for plants and zombies.

Sun will fall from the sky and fungal plants will be asleep.

Front Lawn Night

A Night based lawn with five lanes for plants and zombies.

Sun will not fall from the sky and fungal plants will be awake. Graves will periodically appear spawning even more zombies.

Pool Lawn Day

A Day based level featuring a pool with six lanes.

Sun will fall from the sky, fungal plants will be asleep, and only specific plants can be placed dirrectly on water such as a lilypad.

Pool Lawn Night

A Night level featuring a pool with six lanes.

Sun will not fall from the sky, fungal plants will be awake, and only specific plants can be placed dirrectly on water such as a lilypad.

Roof

A Day level featuring a slanted roof with five lanes.

Sun will fall from the sky, fungal plants will be asleep, and some plants will be unable to fire on the lower part of the slant.

Zen Garden

A greenhouse featuring thirty-two slots for plants.

You will collect seeds during your playthrough to plant in the zen garden. Growing your plants can be a fun break from the normal gameplay and will earn you money for the in-game shop.
