Basic Strategies

In Plants vs. Zombies, you get so many great options of plants to
defend your home, so how do you decide which plants to take into a level?
Well, there are a few important rules to remember.

1. Keep Balance in your Plant Selections!
Make sure to keep balance in your plant types and sun cost. Always bring a sun producing plant
2. Synergy is Important!
Bring plants that work well together, like an ice pea with normal peas or chomper with a walnut.
3. Bring the Right Plants for the Job!
Some plants work better on specific levels or lawns. For example, on the day levels, fungi will be
sleeping so you should either not bring them in your selection or opt to take a coffee bean.
